Fresh pasta elevates any meal, but choosing the right machine can be confusing. Manual rollers give you control and durability, while automatic extruders offer hands-free convenience. Key factors to consider: build material (all-metal vs. plastic), roller width, number of thickness settings, included cutters, and ease of cleaning.
